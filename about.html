About The Artist

Ricardo ‘Cayo’ Candás is a Mexican mathematician and artist with a geometric heart, pirate soul, poet spirit, fighter by instinct, intuitive nature... His mathematical bases help him to mould spaces with unusual geometries and figures with unimaginable symmetries. This bases also permeates his paintings and drawings that could be part of surreal and geometrics worlds. Although his work is almost always independent, I am part of the first edition of Meninas Madrid Gallery 2018.
